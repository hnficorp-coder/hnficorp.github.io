Section 02

Core Competencies

The Data-Driven Advantage

HNFi는 단순히 제품을 공급하지 않습니다. 글로벌 거대 기업의 엄격한 SOP에 최적화된 Retail-Ready Solutions을 제공합니다.

We operate on a proprietary IDMS — Integrated Data Management System, transforming distribution into a precise science. Our framework integrates ingredient control, process certification (HACCP, FDA, CFIA), and rigorous testing & analysis into one unified pipeline.
